Join Our Team

At Green Volunteering Hub, we are dedicated to connecting volunteers with environmental initiatives and organizations across Canada. Our mission is to promote sustainability and environmental conservation through active volunteerism. We are always looking for passionate and talented individuals to join our team and help us make a difference.

Current Openings

Position Description Apply
Volunteer Coordinator As a Volunteer Coordinator, you will be responsible for recruiting, training, and managing volunteers for various environmental projects. Your role will be crucial in ensuring that volunteers are effectively engaged and supported throughout their involvement with our network.
Community Outreach Specialist Our Community Outreach Specialist will work closely with local organizations and communities to promote our volunteer opportunities and build strong partnerships. This role requires excellent communication and networking skills.
Marketing and Communications Manager We are looking for a Marketing and Communications Manager to develop and implement strategies that will increase our visibility and engagement with the public. This role involves managing our social media presence, creating marketing materials, and coordinating public relations efforts.

Why Work With Us?

  • Opportunity to make a positive impact on the environment.
  • Collaborative and inclusive work environment.
  • Professional development and growth opportunities.
  • Competitive salary and benefits package.

How to Apply

If you are passionate about environmental conservation and want to be part of a dynamic team, we would love to hear from you. Please send your resume and a cover letter to careers@greenvolunteernetwork.com with the job title in the subject line.
